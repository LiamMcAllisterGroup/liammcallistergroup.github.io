Welcome

Our research group, lead by Liam McAllister, explores various aspects of string theory phenomenology. We are interested in using string theory to understand the early universe, and in developing string compactifications that lead to realistic four-dimensional physics. In this website you can learn more about our projects, publications, and current members.
